BPimageTranscribe model

The model assets in this directory are converted from `best_model.h5` in
cliffordlab/BPimageTranscribe, released under the GNU General Public License
version 3.

Source: https://github.com/cliffordlab/BPimageTranscribe
Paper: https://doi.org/10.3389/frai.2021.543176
License: https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.html

The model was converted from Keras HDF5 to TensorFlow.js Layers format without
changing its learned weights.
